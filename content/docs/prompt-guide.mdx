A well-written prompt is the difference between a generic image and a striking illustration. This guide breaks down the six elements that make a great prompt, followed by 12 ready-to-use examples with real generated results.

## The Six Elements of a Great Prompt

### 1. Subject

The specific description of who or what is in the frame. Include appearance, clothing, accessories, species, and any distinguishing features.

**Weak:** "A rabbit"
**Strong:** "A small brown rabbit wearing a knitted blue scarf and round brass spectacles"

### 2. Action

What the subject is doing. Action adds narrative energy and makes the scene feel alive rather than static.

**Weak:** "A fox in a forest"
**Strong:** "A fox carefully balancing on a mossy log while reaching for wild blackberries"

### 3. Environment

The physical and visual context — location, architecture, vegetation, weather, season, and surrounding objects.

**Weak:** "In a room"
**Strong:** "Inside a cluttered apothecary shop with shelves of glass jars, dried herbs hanging from the rafters, and sunlight filtering through a dusty window"

### 4. Art Style

The aesthetic direction for the illustration. While TaleCraft already applies a watercolor base style, you can guide the mood and reference point.

**Examples:**
- "Warm and cozy storybook atmosphere"
- "Whimsical and slightly surreal, like a dream sequence"
- "Soft pastoral scene reminiscent of Beatrix Potter"

### 5. Lighting

The quality, direction, mood, and type of light. Lighting dramatically affects the emotional tone of an illustration.

**Examples:**
- "Soft golden afternoon light streaming through a window"
- "Cool blue moonlight with warm candlelight in the foreground"
- "Dappled sunlight filtering through a forest canopy"
- "Warm rim lighting from a crackling fireplace"

### 6. Details

Lens choice, grain, specific graphic elements, textures, and finishing touches that add polish.

**Examples:**
- "Shallow depth of field with the background gently blurred"
- "Visible paper texture and watercolor bleeding at the edges"
- "Tiny details: breadcrumbs on the table, steam rising from the cup"
- "Muted earth-tone palette with pops of warm amber"

## Combining the Elements

A complete prompt brings all six elements together into a single, natural description:

> **Subject:** A young badger in a patchwork apron
> **Action:** stirring a large copper pot of soup
> **Environment:** in a stone cottage kitchen with bundles of dried lavender and a window overlooking a snowy meadow
> **Art Style:** warm and nostalgic storybook illustration
> **Lighting:** golden evening light from the fireplace casting long shadows
> **Details:** visible watercolor texture, steam curling from the pot, a cat sleeping by the hearth

**Combined prompt:**
"A young badger in a patchwork apron stirring a large copper pot of soup in a stone cottage kitchen, bundles of dried lavender on the walls and a window overlooking a snowy meadow, warm and nostalgic storybook illustration style, golden evening fireplace light casting long shadows, visible watercolor texture with steam curling from the pot and a cat sleeping by the hearth"
